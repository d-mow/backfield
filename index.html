Why a studio

Backfield started as a way to stop losing things: lineups on napkins, hours in my head, punch lists living in photos.

I'm an architect during the week and a dad at the ballpark on the weekend — both jobs are mostly details.

The apps are the things I kept wishing I could find, built mostly out of frustration with big companies that have no taste.

One file each, offline, nothing to sign up for. Stats, field, reference, organize, design.
